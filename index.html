About BHS

We connect great businesses with great people and great food.

Founded with a passion for service excellence, Bheem's Hiring & Catering Services (BHS) is a dual-service business dedicated to making every event and workplace run smoothly.

On the recruitment side, we connect talented individuals with great employers — offering job placement, career consulting, and candidate sourcing.

On the catering side, we craft menus that delight, using fresh ingredients and recipes that turn any gathering into a memorable experience.
