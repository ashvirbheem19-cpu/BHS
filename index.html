About BHS

We connect great businesses with great people and great food.

Founded with a passion for service excellence, Bheem's Hiring Services and Catering (BHS) is a dual-service business dedicated to making every event and workplace run smoothly.

On the hire side, we supply quality event equipment — from marquees, chairs, and tables to cutlery and glassware. We visit your venue to take measurements before providing a tailored quote.

On the catering side, we prepare delicious meals in our kitchen and deliver them to your venue — ready to serve, no on-site cooking needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

What areas do you serve?

We serve across KZN, including Durban, Pietermaritzburg, Ballito, Umhlanga, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm we cover your location.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance, especially during peak wedding season. However, we always do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

Is there a minimum order?

Not at all. Whether you need 20 chairs or 200, we're happy to help with any size event.

Can I hire equipment and order catering together?

Absolutely. We specialise in combining both services for a seamless event experience — one booking, one delivery, one team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EFT and cash. A deposit is required to secure your booking, with the balance due before or on event day.
